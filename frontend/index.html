Data Usage & Privacy

ISS Spotter uses your location information strictly to calculate when the International Space Station will be visible from your area.

We collect your approximate geographic coordinates (latitude and longitude) only after you sign in and grant permission. This data is stored securely and is never shared with third parties.

Your email address is used solely for authentication and, if you choose, to notify you on days when the ISS is visible from your location. You can opt out of notifications at any time.

We do not track your movement, do not sell data, and do not collect any information unrelated to ISS visibility predictions.
